Temperature development for the meterological spring (Mar-Apr-May)

The visualization depicts the Northern Hemisphere metereological spring temperature changes (ie the MAM values) from the GISTEMP data set throughout the years.

Data from GISTEMP Team, 2015: GISS Surface Temperature Analysis (GISTEMP). NASA Goddard Institute for Space Studies. Dataset accessed end of July 2015 at http://data.giss.nasa.gov/gistemp/.

Hansen, J., R. Ruedy, M. Sato, and K. Lo, 2010: Global surface temperature change, Rev. Geophys., 48, RG4004, doi:10.1029/2010RG000345.
